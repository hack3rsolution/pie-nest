“The magpie builds its nest everywhere — yet never forgets where it comes from.”

“Pie Nest” is named after the magpie — a bird that symbolizes good fortune in Korean folklore, yet thrives on every continent. It’s a fitting metaphor for what we do.

We believe Korean culture holds wisdom worth sharing: ancient frameworks for understanding ourselves, a language shaped by centuries of philosophy, and daily practices that bring clarity to modern life. Our apps are the bridges.

How we build

Three principles that guide everything we create.

Culture first, technology second

We start with deep cultural research — consulting practitioners, reading original texts, and understanding the ‘why’ behind traditions — before writing a single line of code.

AI as a translator, not a replacement

Our AI doesn’t invent wisdom. It translates centuries of cultural knowledge into language and insights that feel relevant to your life today, in your language, in your context.

Ship fast, listen faster

We believe in rapid iteration driven by real user feedback. Every feature we build is shaped by people who actually use our apps, not assumptions made in a vacuum.
